The Studio

Built for families, inspired by our community.

Why I Became a Chiropractor

A passion for helping people achieve their health goals.

From discovering chiropractic in secondary school to practicing for over 16 years, my journey has always been about supporting families in reaching their health goals. There’s nothing better than seeing people thrive, from babies to grandparents!

Family Care at Its Best

Supporting families through every stage of life.

After my own experiences with pregnancy and motherhood, I’m even more committed to helping families thrive. Whether you’re navigating pregnancy or balancing life with young children, I’ve been there and I’m here to support you. Let’s work together to achieve your health goals.

Who I Serve

The Thrive Studio has been designed for families who value efficiency, connection, and compassionate care. Pregnancy, Mum & Baby

From preconception to postnatal, chiropractic care offers a safe and gentle way to promote comfort and minimise back pain in pregnancy. As a mum of two, I truly understand the high's and low's of this journey and I’m here to help you manage the changes that come with this special time. After birth, I also provide gentle checks for your baby and support for the new challenges of feeding, lifting, and carrying.

Toddlers, Kids & Teens

As babies grow and start to develop their spinal curves, crawl, and walk, we can help with the little bumps and falls along the way, making sure their spines and muscles are working at their best. For teenagers, sudden growth spurts, sports injuries, heavy school bags, and long hours hunched over desks or computers may take a toll. Chiropractic care can be a great way to support them through these changes too.

Adults & Older Years

Between commuting, work and family stress, long hours at a desk, gym workouts, weekend jogs, and that all important golf tournament, it’s easy to feel the strain. That’s where chiropractic care comes in, helping you stay active and keep up with everything you love to do. Thrive Family Chiropractic is here for your whole family, from relieving minor aches to addressing long-term wellness.

Chiropractic For The Whole Family
