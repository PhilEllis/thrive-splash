Cookie Policy

At Thrive Family Chiropractic our websites use cookies and similar technologies (collectively “cookies”) to ensure the sites function correctly, to improve performance, and—where you consent—for analytics and marketing purposes. Cookies may also be placed by third parties.

What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device and remember your preferences.

Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary cookies

These are essential for the websites to function (e.g. to store your cookie preferences). They do not require your consent.

Functional cookies

These remember your choices (e.g. language or display settings) to improve your experience. We use Usercentrics CookieBot - Stores cookie consent settings for 365 days

Statistics cookies

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our websites. This helps us improve usability. Analytics cookies are not set until you consent via the banner.

Marketing cookies

These may be set by third-party services (e.g. Facebook social plugins) to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes.

Practice Hub

When you leave our websites to book via Practice Hub, their own cookies and privacy practices apply. Please see Practice Hub’s Privacy Policy for.

Consent

On your first visit you will see a cookie banner. By selecting your preferences and clicking “Apply”, you consent to the categories of cookies you have chosen. You can withdraw or change your consent at any time via the banner link in the footer.

Managing cookies

You can also control and delete cookies through your browser settings. If you disable all cookies, parts of the websites may not work properly.

Your data rights

You have rights under UK GDPR, including to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. For details, see our Privacy Policy. You can also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).
